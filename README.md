# #44 Simple Mirror

A hyper-minimalist, browser-based digital mirror. No cameras to snap, no filters to choose—just a pure reflection designed for the modern minimalist.

---

## 1. Concept
"Simple Mirror" (Fridge Combo #44) focuses on the absolute essentials of a physical mirror. It eliminates the friction of native camera apps by launching an instant, full-screen, horizontally-flipped view the moment you open the URL.

## 2. Key Features
* **Zero-UI Experience:** No shutter buttons, no zoom sliders, no distractions.
* **Light Frame Design:** The white border serves as a natural ring light, illuminating your face in dim environments.
* **True Reflection:** Forced horizontal flip (`scaleX(-1)`) to mimic the behavior of a real physical mirror.
* **One-Tap Immersive Mode:** Tap anywhere to enter/exit fullscreen and hide browser toolbars.
* **Privacy First:** No images are saved, no data is tracked. Browser-only local processing.

## 3. Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** Vanilla HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript.
* **API:** `MediaDevices.getUserMedia()` for real-time camera stream.
* **Performance:** Optimized for low-spec mobile and desktop browsers.

## 4. Design System
* **Background:** `#fff`
* **Main Color:** `#333`
* **Border Radius:** `15px` (Mandatory)
* **Typography:** System Sans-serif (Bold/Tool-like UI)
